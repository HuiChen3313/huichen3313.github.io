Bio

I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from Macquarie University, co-supervised by Prof. Longbing Cao and Prof. Jinyan Li, after beginning my doctoral studies at the University of Technology Sydney. My research spans probabilistic machine learning, Bayesian deep learning, federated learning, and temporal point processes, with a focus on developing reliable, uncertainty-aware, and scalable models for distributed and spatio-temporal data environments.

I have authored or co-authored more than 20 research publications in leading journals and conferences, including Artificial Intelligence, TNNLS, Information Fusion, Expert Systems with Applications, IJCAI, and KDD.

I am actively engaged in the research community, serving as a Session Chair for PAKDD 2025 and as a Program Committee member for major conferences such as NeurIPS 2025, ICLR 2025, IJCAI 2025, KDD 2026, ACML 2025, ECML-PKDD 2025, and DSAA 2024. I also review for top-tier journals including TNNLS, TCYB, TMLR, DMKD, and npj Digital Medicine. I received the HDR Research Rising Star Award in 2025 for my research contributions.
