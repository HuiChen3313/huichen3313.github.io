Bio

I am currently a PhD student in the School of Computing at Macquarie University, co-supervised by Prof. Longbing Cao and Prof. Jinyan Li. Prior to transferring to Macquarie University, I commenced my PhD at the University of Technology Sydney. My research interests include Probabilistic Machine Learning and Bayesian Statistics.
